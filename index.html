About Me

I am a PhD researcher focusing on Intrusion Detection Systems using Graph Neural Networks and Federated Learning. My research involves developing novel approaches to cybersecurity in IoT and CPS environments, with a particular emphasis on privacy-preserving distributed learning techniques.

Current Research

FDSG: Federated Framework with Dynamic Similarity attention using Graph neural networks

Developing an innovative approach to intrusion detection combining Federated Learning with Graph Neural Networks, focusing on:

  • Integration of GAT and GraphSAGE for enhanced network analysis
  • Privacy-preserving distributed learning techniques
  • Dynamic similarity metrics for improved detection accuracy
  • Scalable solutions for IoT and CPS environments
